1 – In the boot grub menu, select (e) to edit.

2 – Go to the line starting with 'Linux 16' and change 'ro' with 'rw init=/sysroot/bin/sh'

3 – Now press Control+x to start on single user mode.
This will take a while: "Generating "/run/initramfs/rdsosrepot.txt"

4 – Now access the system with:
chroot /sysroot

5 – Reset root password
passwd root

6 – Update selinux information
touch /.autorelabel

7 – Exit chroot
exit

8 – Reboot your system
reboot
